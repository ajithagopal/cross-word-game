Crossword

What is it?

A word puzzle where you solve clues and fill in the correct words into a grid, both across and down.

How to Play

  • Click a clue to highlight its row or column.
  • Type your answer — one letter per box.
  • Use arrow keys or tap to navigate between boxes.
  • Solve all clues to complete the puzzle.

Tip to Start

Start with clues you're confident about or shorter ones — they often reveal letters that help solve tougher clues.
