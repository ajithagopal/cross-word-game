Sudoku

What is it?

A number puzzle where you fill a 9×9 grid so that each row, column, and 3×3 box contains all digits from 1 to 9 without repetition.

How to Play

  • Tap or click a cell and enter a number.
  • Ensure no number repeats in any row, column, or 3×3 box.
  • Use pencil mode or notes for possible entries.

Tip to Start

Begin with rows, columns, or boxes that already have more numbers — they’re easier to complete first.
